About Kaori Tsuji

Born in Tokyo. Started modeling career in her 20’s in Paris and appeared the most prestigious Paris collections such as Hermès, Christian Dior, CARVEN, agnes b, JC de CASTELBAJAC etc... in 1998 Appearance in France World Cup Yves Saint Laurent show.
In 2002, found out her talent by great French director Alain Corneau and made her film debut as main actress in 'Stupeur et Tremblements'.
After 10 years of living in Paris, published first essay book 'Parisienne to Taxi no Hosoku' from points of view of various experiences, and has been actively engaging in diverse fields.
